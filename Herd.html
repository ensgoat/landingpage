ENS Domains (.eth)

ENS (Ethereum Name Service) is a decentralized system that allows users to register and use human-readable names for their Ethereum addresses. These names, known as ENS domains, are short and easy to remember, making it easier for users to send and receive transactions on the Ethereum network. They can be used to identify and interact with smart contracts, as well as to send and receive payments on the Ethereum network.
